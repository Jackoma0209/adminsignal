This article breaks down the real differences between the two versions from an admin perspective.

## Key Differences in 2026

### 1. AI and Copilot+ Experience
Windows 11 26H1 brings meaningful improvements to on-device AI features. If you're deploying Copilot+ PCs or planning to use local AI capabilities (Recall, Live Captions, Studio Effects, etc.), 26H1 offers better performance and integration.

25H2 still supports these features but with more limitations on older hardware.

### 2. Performance and Battery Life
Microsoft has continued optimising the kernel and power management in 26H1. Many admins report noticeably better battery life and lower idle CPU usage on 26H1 compared to 25H2, especially on newer Intel and AMD hardware.

### 3. Security Improvements
26H1 includes additional memory safety improvements and enhanced Credential Guard protections. While both versions are secure, 26H1 has a stronger default security posture out of the box.

### 4. Intune and Autopilot Experience
Both versions work excellently with Intune and Autopilot. However, 26H1 includes some quality-of-life improvements in the enrollment experience and better reporting in the Intune portal for newer devices.

## Upgrade Considerations for Admins

### When You Should Consider Windows 11 26H1

- You're buying **new hardware** in 2026 (especially Copilot+ PCs)
- You want the **best battery life and performance** on modern devices
- Your organisation is increasing use of **on-device AI features**
- You prefer to stay on the newer servicing branch

### When Windows 11 25H2 Is Still a Good Choice

- You have a large existing fleet on 24H2/25H2 and want to minimise change
- You're not heavily using Copilot+ features yet
- You prefer to wait for 26H1 to mature (typical advice for the first 3–6 months after release)
- Your hardware is older and may not benefit significantly from 26H1

## Recommended Approach in June 2026

| Scenario                                      | Recommended Version | Reason |
|-----------------------------------------------|---------------------|--------|
| New device purchases                          | **26H1**            | Better long-term support and features |
| Existing stable environments                  | **25H2**            | Lower risk, proven stability |
| Heavy Copilot+ / AI usage                     | **26H1**            | Improved on-device AI performance |
| Conservative / highly regulated environments  | **25H2**            | More mature and widely tested |
| Planning major refresh in next 12 months      | **26H1**            | Future-proofing |

## Final Recommendation

As of June 2026:

- **New deployments** → Go with **Windows 11 26H1**
- **Existing stable environments** → Stay on **25H2** for now and plan a controlled move to 26H1 later in the year or in 2027

Most organisations will benefit from running a mixed environment during the second half of 2026 while they evaluate 26H1 in pilot groups.
